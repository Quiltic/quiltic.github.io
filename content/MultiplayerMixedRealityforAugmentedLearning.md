This is my Masters of Engineering Capstone project for CS at University of Cincinnati.

This project is focused around the concept of having multiple Mixed Reality (MR) headsets active for use of teaching through play and example.

# Intro

- Uses Mixed Reality with the Meta Quest system for passthrough.
- Written in both Unreal and Unity for quality comparison.
- Simple interface for ease of use.
- Current scope is to demonstrate geological specimens with no physical objects to allow for easier viewing and explanations.
- Designed for simple setup, connection, augmentation, and usage.
- No AI/ML usage.
> While this may not sound like an important point. Most new projects in the CS field are focused around Machine Learning. Unfortunately this trend may continue into the future. However, I chose to not include ML for this process as teaching is not something that I want ML to be involved in. The lack of humanity when teaching would be a tremendous loss for everyone.
