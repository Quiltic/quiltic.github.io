# Background

So about me, I am a Computer Science student who's gone to the University of Cincincnati for many, many years. I've gotten both my Bachelors and Masters from them, and I primarily am interested in the weirder side of CS. Not the technical side in the idea of making new algorithms that do compression or whatnot. But, the things that are adjacent to CS or used by CS that aren't actually explicitly CS. For instance, my Capstone is all about Mixed Reality, having both the real world and the virtual world combined. This isn't exactly something that comes to mind immediately when you say Computer Science, but it is something that I have worked very hard on. 
  
The other fields that I'm interested are bioinformatics. The idea that medical teams are getting so much data that they just can't parse it by themselves anymore and since the medical field is an extremely important field, I think that that's really cool and interesting area to work in. I haven't actually done any major projects with bioinformatics aside from taking a few classes. While I am a little disappointed by that fact, I have also been interested in an area called Synthetic Biology. 
  
Synthetic Biology is the idea that creatures can be made synthetically. Making raw creatures from basically nothing has been really interesting to me, and I've been keeping a close eye on that.
