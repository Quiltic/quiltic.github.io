Hello! This is a super simple "board game". 

# Get Started

This game requires 2 simple things. **Something stackable** (called Stones) and **a spot away from prying eyes.**

## Recommended Stones
- Flat Rocks
- Coins
- Dice
- Legos
- Chips

## Players: 2-6 
Untested with more than 6.

## 2-4 Min Game

# Rules

**1:** Players decide how many "Stones" they will use for the game.
> 6-8 is good for a quick game.
>
> 9-11 for a medium game
>
> 12+ for a longer game

**2:** Players then make and hide any number of Towers with their Stones.
> Make sure to hide the Towers from other players!

**3.1:** Players take turns guessing the size of at least one of another players Towers.
- You can only pick one player at a time when guessing.
- Loser of the last game goes first. (Otherwise oldest goes first.) 
**3.2:** If the player guesses a Tower size that their target doesn't have they lose a Stone from one of their Towers.
**3.3:** If the player guesses a Tower size that their target has, then the target loses a Stone.

**4:** When a player loses a Stone they may pick from which Tower it is removed from.

**5:** When a player no-longer has any Towers they lose, and can no-longer guess. 

**6:** The winner is the last player with a Tower standing.

# Example Play

For this example we will be picking **3** people named Bernard (B), Noel (N), and Teak (T) and **6 Stones**.
- Bernard has 3 towers with sizes: 1,2,3
- Noel has 2 towers: 3,3
- Teak has 4 towers: 1,1,1,1,2

Starting with Bernard. They will pick Noel and say 2. Being **Wrong** B will lose 1 Stone and pick the 1 Tower (removing from the game).

Noel will pick Teak saying 1. N being **Correct** will make T lose 1 Stone, which they will remove a 1 Tower.

T picks B with 3. B removes from 3 (making a tower of 2). B now has Towers of 2,2.

B picks N with 3. N remove from 3. (N: 2,3)
> As of B's Last turn the group looks like:
>
> B: 2,2
>
> N: 2,3
> 
> T: 1,1,1,2

N->T: 1. T => 1,1,2

T->N: 1. T => 1,2

B->N: 3. N => 1,3

N->T: 1. T => 2

T->B: 2. B => 1,2

B->T: 1. B => 2

N->B: 1. N => 3

T->N: 2. T => 1

B->T: 1. **Teak Out**

N->B: 2. B => 1

B->N: 3. N => 2

N->B: 1. **Bernard Out**

**Noel Wins!**
